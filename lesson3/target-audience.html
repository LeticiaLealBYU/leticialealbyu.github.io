Target Audience Description

Our target audience cover a great variety of people, we are interested on people of any age that love outdoor activities, it can be playing sports, visiting parks, photographing landscapes and so on. The website can be accessed by computer, tablet or smartphone.

Persona 1

22 years old, single, male. He is finishing his bachelor’s degree in graphic design and is a photographer at a advertising agency. He loves to take pictures of landscapes on different types of weather and seeks to know what the weather conditions are so that he can plan the best pictures. He uses the computer and the smartphone to access the website

Persona 2

31 years old,maried, female. She is the onwer and instructor of a small hiking company, she loves adventures. When she is not hiking for work she is hiking or doing some other outside activity with her family. She constantly checks the weather to plan her trips and be a carefull instructor. She mainly accesses the website over her smartphone.

Scenarios

  • Will I need to bring an umbrella?
  • Will I be able to do my trip?
  • Will it be too dangerous to take pictures where I planned?
  • Will my customer be able to have the hiking lessons next week?
  • Will it be snowing for me to that the best christmas pictures?
